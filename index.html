Privacy Policy

Extension: Transcribe Video to Text
Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Overview

Transcribe Video to Text is a Chrome browser extension that transcribes and summarizes audio and video content. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

  • Audio data: When you click Transcribe, audio from the active tab is temporarily captured and sent to Deepgram for processing. Audio is not stored after transcription.
  • Usage credits: We store a usage counter per session to enforce the free-tier limit. No personal data is stored.
  • YouTube video metadata: Video ID and title are read from the page to identify the video. Not transmitted to our servers.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not store transcription results on our servers.
  • We do not track browsing history outside of active transcription sessions.
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

Audio transcription is powered by Deepgram. Audio is transmitted solely for transcription and is subject to their privacy policy.

5. Permissions

  • tabCapture / offscreen: To capture tab audio for transcription.
  • activeTab: To read the current page URL and video metadata.
  • storage: To store your credit balance locally in the browser.
  • sidePanel: To display the transcription interface.

6. Data Security

All audio data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. We do not retain audio after transcription is complete.
